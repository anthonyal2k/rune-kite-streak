# Rune Kite Streak

RuneLite plugin that tracks your consecutive Wilderness Agility Course laps that result in a Rune Kiteshield drop. 

## Features
* **Streak Tracking:** Counts consecutive laps that drop a Rune Kiteshield from the Wilderness Agility Dispenser.
* **Best Streak:** Remembers your longest streak for the session.
* **Chat Announcements:** Optionally broadcasts your current and best streaks directly into your local game chat.
* **Auto-Reset:** Automatically breaks and resets your streak to zero if you complete a lap without receiving a Rune Kiteshield.
